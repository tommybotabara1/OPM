help Frequently Asked Questions


1. Can I view my account using my phone?
Yes, our system is accessible in mobile devices as long as you have internet connection.

2. Am I required to have internet connection when recording my vitals?
Yes, as much as possible, so that the results can be viewed easily on your account.

3. Can I update my password and personal information using this account?
Yes, you can update them on the Manage Account page. You can find it under the Modules tab.

4. Can I restrict the access of my data to other users?
Yes, you can do it here on the Restrict Access page. You can also find it under the Modules tab.

5. Can I change my doctor?
No, but you can allow a doctor that has an account for this system to access and view your data. You can also do it here on the Restrict Access page.

6. Can I create a new account in this system?
No, only the admin can create accounts for users.

7. Can I let others access my account for me?
As much as possible, we prefer if you are the only one who can access your account. Please be mindful that you are the one in control of your own sensitive information. So in case your data are accessed by others, you are the one responsible for giving them access.

8. As for the 5000 PHP collateral money, will I be able to get it back once I am done using the system and the device?
Yes. The payment will only be refundable IF AND ONLY IF the device is returned safely and does not have any damages. We will be checking first as soon as you return it to us.

The payment will NOT be refundable in cases such as:
  1. Device gets broken while being used. You are required to bring the device to the hospital for it to be replaced by a new one. Payment will not be returned anymore since it will be used for the broken device.
  2. You were not able to return the device back on its due date.
9. What if I do not give back my device on its due date?
We will be contacting you about it. You are required to return the device at the required date set by the doctor that is indicated in the consent form.

10. Will I be able to access my data using my account forever? - di ako sure haha so double check.
Yes. Although if an account is inactive after a month, the account is up for reactivation to the admin.
